The Campaign

How 115 repos get built

Methodical, strategic, compounding. Each milestone unlocked the next.

2023 — Origin
Identified the gap
M-PESA had no AI layer. Kenya's 47M Swahili speakers had no tools in their language. First tools built: daraja-ai, shule-ai, basic civic tools. Realized the problem wasn't one app — it was missing infrastructure.
2024 — Infrastructure First
mpesa-mcp ships on PyPI
First MCP server for East Africa. Model Context Protocol was weeks old — mpesa-mcp made M-PESA the first African payment API natively accessible to AI agents. 400+ downloads across 12 countries without any marketing.
2025 — Protocol Trifecta
MCP + A2A + Google ADK — all three, first in region
kenya-a2a and kenya-adk implemented within weeks of global launches. First documented engineer in East Africa to have deployed all three major AI agent protocols. Portfolio crosses 50 repositories.
2026 — Domain Saturation
110 repositories. Complete coverage.
Every major domain covered: financial, health, legal, civic, agriculture, education, housing, transport, trade, environment, mental health, emergency response, refugee services, informal sector. LINGUA Africa grant application submitted to Microsoft AI for Good Lab × Gates Foundation.
2026 — Research-Backed
5 arXiv/IMF/WHO papers → 5 East Africa firsts
Systematic scan of global research — arXiv, IMF Working Papers, WHO guidelines, PLOS One. Built 5 tools with no equivalent in East Africa: Swahili crop disease detection (PlantVillage), county economic nowcasting (IMF NTL methodology), constitutional rights multi-agent debate (Liang et al. 2023), diaspora remittance MCP server (World Bank RPW), and Swahili CHW clinical co-pilot (federated AI for African health).
Next — Scale
Community validation and adoption
User studies in Nairobi. Swahili model fine-tuning (LINGUA Africa grant, June 15 deadline). Digital Public Goods registration. Expanding to Tanzania, Uganda, Rwanda — same infrastructure, same protocols, new context.
